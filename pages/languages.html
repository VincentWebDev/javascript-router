2. Python

  • Job Growth Rate: Continues to rise with the increasing importance of data analytics and AI.
  • Average Salary for Junior Developers: Projected to be around $70,000 - $90,000 annually.

3. SQL

  • Overview: SQL is the standard language for managing and manipulating databases. It's crucial for data analysis and backend development.
  • Job Growth Rate: Steady growth as data-driven decision-making becomes more prevalent across industries.
  • Average Salary for Junior Developers: Expected to be around $55,000 - $75,000 annually.

4. Swift

  • Overview: Developed by Apple, Swift is used for iOS and macOS applications. It's known for its speed and modern syntax.
  • Job Growth Rate: Increasing as the demand for iOS applications grows.
  • Average Salary for Junior Developers: Expected to be around $70,000 - $90,000 annually.
